Grand Theft Auto: Vice City Stories is a 2006 action-adventure game developed by Rockstar Leeds and Rockstar North for the PlayStation Portable. Set in 1984, it serves as a prequel to Grand Theft Auto: Vice City, following Victor "Vic" Vance as he builds a criminal empire amidst Vice City's power struggles. The game introduces an "empire building" system, allowing players to take over businesses and expand their influence. Praised for its engaging storyline and vibrant open world, Vice City Stories has sold over 4.5 million copies, making it one of the best-selling PSP games.
